IT Support Message Templates
============================

Use these templates when opening a network/VLAN ticket.

Template 1: Desktop on wrong VLAN
---------------------------------
Hello IT Team,

I am connecting over VPN and cannot SSH to my desktop.

Diagnostic details:
- VPN is connected.
- My desktop appears to be on VLAN 1 / 10.1.x.x (or another non-routed subnet).
- VPN routes available from my side are for 10.10.x.x and 10.156.x.x.

Request:
Please move this desktop to the routed VLAN/subnet used for remote access
(for example VLAN 10 / 10.10.x.x), or confirm the correct routed network.

Thank you.

Template 2: SSH port unreachable
--------------------------------
Hello IT Team,

I can reach the desktop host, but SSH (port 22) is not reachable from VPN.

Diagnostic details:
- VPN is connected.
- Ping status: reachable.
- SSH port check: closed/filtered on port 22.

Request:
Please verify firewall/network policy for SSH on this host and confirm if
port 22 should be open from the VPN network.

Thank you.

Template 3: VPN route issue
---------------------------
Hello IT Team,

I am connected to VPN, but cannot reach the desktop subnet from my client.

Diagnostic details:
- VPN interface is up.
- Route checks do not show expected paths to the desktop subnet.
- Desktop address: <replace-with-desktop-ip>

Request:
Please verify that routes for this subnet are correctly advertised to VPN
clients and confirm expected reachability.

Thank you.
